# Enhanced Meeting Reminders

**Requested by:** Farm Bureau (Steve Love)
**Date:** 2026-02-26
**Source:** Demo meeting

## The Ask
Multiple reminders before scheduled meetings: day before, hours before, not just the standard calendar reminder.

## The Need
Workers comp claimants aren't sitting at desks. They're:
- In cornfields operating combines
- On construction sites
- At remote job locations

A 5-minute Outlook reminder doesn't help someone who needs to stop what they're doing and find a place to take a video call. They need advance notice to plan.

## Context
Steve compared this to dentist appointment reminders:
> "Not only is it showing up on your calendar, but then a day before you're being reminded, hey, by golly, tomorrow you've got an appointment. And then we all know how we get busy during our day. A few hours before the appointment, just another reminder."

Current system relies on Outlook calendar reminders, which:
- Require the person to be at their computer
- Don't work well for field workers
- Don't help if someone doesn't add it to their calendar

## Suggested Implementation
- Day before: Email/SMS reminder
- Morning of: Email/SMS reminder (if afternoon appointment)
- 1-2 hours before: SMS reminder
- Make reminder schedule configurable per organization

## Priority Signals
- [x] Multiple customers asked — Common need for any field-based workforce
- [x] Blocking a deal — Directly impacts meeting show rates
- [ ] Causing churn risk
- [ ] Competitive pressure

## Notes
This would improve meeting show rates significantly. No-shows are a real problem when scheduling with claimants who don't work desk jobs.
