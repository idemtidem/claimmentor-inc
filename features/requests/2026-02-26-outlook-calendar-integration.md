**Requested by:** Farm Bureau (Brett Sirmex)
**Date:** 2026-02-26

## The Ask
Integration with Outlook calendar for scheduling recorded statements.

## The Need

## Context

- Their adjusters use Outlook for scheduling
- Reducing friction in scheduling = higher adoption
