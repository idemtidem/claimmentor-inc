## Slide 3: The Surveillance Problem, Solved

**The challenge Steve described:**
> "Surveillance doesn't have value if you cannot identify the worker."

Construction workers, depot employees — often no photo on file.

**With ClaimMentor:**
- Every recorded statement captures video
- SIU can identify the claimant for surveillance
- Supports fraud detection on PTD claims

*Video isn't just convenience — it's evidence.*

---

## Slide 4: Morphing Injuries

**The problem:**
> "You've got a worker that originally presents for a lumbar injury. Three to four months later, it's like, yeah, my shoulder hurt too."

**With ClaimMentor:**
- Full transcript of original statement
- Search for body parts mentioned
- Compare to later claims
- Share with claimant: "Here's what you said on record"

*Medical cost containment through documentation.*
